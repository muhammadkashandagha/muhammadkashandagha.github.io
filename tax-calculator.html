ℹ️ About the PK Income Tax Calculator

The PK Income Tax Calculator helps salaried individuals estimate their income tax under the latest FBR tax rates. It provides monthly and annual tax estimates, take-home salary, effective tax rates, and historical comparisons, making it easier to understand how tax liabilities change over time.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is this calculator free?

Yes. The calculator is completely free to use.

Which tax years are supported?

Currently, the calculator supports Finance Act 2020 through the latest Finance Act 2026-2027.

Is the calculation accurate?

The calculator is based on the applicable FBR salary tax slabs. It provides an estimate and should not replace professional tax advice.

Does it calculate tax for freelancers?

No. It is designed for salaried individuals only.

Is Zakat included?

No.

Are tax credits considered?

No. Personal tax credits, rebates, and exemptions are not included.

How often is the calculator updated?

The calculator is updated whenever new Finance Acts become effective.
