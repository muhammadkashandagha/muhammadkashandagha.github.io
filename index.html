About Me

Who I Am & What Drives Me

Helping SMEs and growing businesses in Karachi and across Pakistan build stronger finance functions with structure and clarity.

I am Muhammad Kashan Dagha (MKD), ACA (ICAP), a Practicing Chartered Accountant and Management Consultant based in Karachi, Pakistan, helping SMEs and growing businesses build stronger finance functions.

My career began in audit and assurance, where I led engagements across multiple industries under International Standards on Auditing (ISA). This experience gave me a deep understanding of financial reporting, internal controls, risk assessment, and regulatory compliance.

Today, I work closely with business owners in Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, and across Pakistan as a trusted finance partner, helping them bring structure, clarity, and discipline to their finance operations.

My areas of expertise include:

  • Financial reporting and IFRS advisory
  • Bookkeeping and accounting system implementation
  • Internal controls and process improvement
  • Management reporting and performance dashboards
  • Income tax and sales tax compliance in Pakistan
  • Fractional Finance Manager services for SMEs

I believe accounting is more than compliance. Numbers should help business owners make better decisions, manage risks confidently, and grow sustainably.

My goal is simple: to provide financial clarity and peace of mind so that business owners can focus on building their vision while I take care of the numbers.
